about

Software engineering is structured thinking applied to unstructured problems.

That principle has guided every line of code I have written -- from early experiments with JavaScript to architecting production systems at the Fortune 1 company.

Today, as a Software Developer at Walmart, I work across the full stack to deliver high-impact features for one of the world's largest digital retail platforms. My work involves designing scalable APIs, optimizing frontend performance, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to ship features that serve 240 million customers weekly.

I specialize in React ecosystems, Node.js services, and performance optimization. I have contributed to systems that handle traffic spikes, edge cases, and the unpredictable reality of enterprise-scale commerce.
