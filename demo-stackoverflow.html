The Problem

The Solution

  backend: {
    api: "Python FastAPI for ML endpoints",
    streaming: "Apache Flink for stream processing",

    ml: "TensorFlow models for demand forecasting"

    dataPoints: "2.4M data points processed per minute",

  • Reduced data latency from 4-6 hours to under 3 seconds

  • Estimated $12M annual savings from reduced stockouts and overstock
