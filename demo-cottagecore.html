Chapter One

About the Gardener

In which we meet our protagonist

Hello and welcome. I'm Anshull Garg, a software developer who believes that the best code grows much like a garden -- with patience, care, and a respect for the ecosystem it lives within.

At Walmart, I tend to large-scale systems that serve millions of people every day. I find deep satisfaction in nurturing clean architecture, cultivating robust APIs, and pruning away complexity until what remains is simple, resilient, and beautiful.

When I'm not at the keyboard, you'll find me exploring the natural patterns that inspire good software design -- because the best architectures have always been grown, not merely built.

~ Written from a sunlit desk, surrounded by houseplants ~
