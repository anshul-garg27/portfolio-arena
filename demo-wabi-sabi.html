About 紹介

Software is rarely perfect at birth. Like aged wood or weathered stone, the best code carries the marks of its journey -- refactored, refined, reshaped by real-world pressures until it finds its natural form.

I build systems at Walmart that serve millions, embracing the tension between scale and simplicity. Every architecture decision is a conversation between ambition and restraint. I find the cracks are where the most interesting solutions live.

My work spans the full stack, but my heart lives in the spaces between -- the integrations, the data flows, the invisible seams that hold everything together.

"In the world of technology, impermanence is the only constant. Embrace it."
  • Thoughtful over clever
  • Resilient over rigid
  • Clear over complex
  • Humble over heroic
  • Sustainable over fast
