Feature Story

Software engineering, at its core, is an exercise in structured thinking applied to unstructured problems. That principle has guided every line of code Anshull Garg has written -- from early experiments with JavaScript to architecting production systems at the Fortune 1 company.

Today, as a Software Developer at Walmart, Garg works across the full stack to deliver high-impact features for one of the world's largest digital retail platforms. His day-to-day involves designing scalable APIs, optimizing frontend performance, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to ship features that serve 240 million customers weekly.

He specializes in React ecosystems, Node.js services, and performance optimization. He has contributed to systems that handle traffic spikes, edge cases, and the unpredictable reality of enterprise-scale commerce.

Checkout Pipeline Optimization

Walmart Inc. -- 2025

Led a comprehensive optimization initiative targeting Walmart's checkout flow -- one of the highest-traffic pages in global e-commerce. The project involved deep performance profiling, strategic code splitting, and a complete rearchitecture of the client-side rendering pipeline.

Reduced page load time by 35% and decreased JavaScript bundle size by 40% through lazy loading, tree shaking, and migrating critical rendering paths to server-side execution. Implemented optimistic UI updates that cut perceived latency for payment processing by half.
